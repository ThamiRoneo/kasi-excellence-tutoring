Mathematics
GRADE 8–12 & ADULT BASIC EDUCATION · CAPS ALIGNED

From foundational number skills to complex calculus, our Mathematics programme meets learners exactly where they are and builds them up systematically. Every session is tailored to your grade, your gaps, and your upcoming exams.

  • Foundation Phase & Primary School support
  • Grade 8–9 Intermediate level
  • Grade 10–12 Matric preparation
  • Adult Basic Education (ABE) programme
  • Past paper practice & marking
  • Exam technique & time management coaching
CAPS Note: All content is aligned to the current South African CAPS curriculum. We track NCS annual teaching plans to ensure you never fall behind on the prescribed topics.
Subject 02 · #physical-sciences
⚗️
Physical Sciences
GRADE 10–12 · PHYSICS & CHEMISTRY · EXAM PREPARATION

Physical Sciences combines Physics and Chemistry — two subjects many learners find intimidating. We break them down into understandable, real-world concepts that click. Our tutors use local examples and analogies to make abstract theory feel familiar.

  • Physics modules for Grade 10, 11 & 12
  • Chemistry modules for Grade 10, 11 & 12
  • Practical experiments explained & demystified
  • High-frequency matric exam topics
  • Formula sheets & memorisation strategies
  • Paper 1 (Physics) & Paper 2 (Chemistry) prep
Exam Focus: We concentrate heavily on the topics that appear most in matric exams — electricity & magnetism, waves, chemical equilibrium, and organic chemistry — ensuring maximum return on study time.
Subject 03 · #accounting
💰
Accounting
GRADE 10–12 · FINANCIAL LITERACY · PRACTICAL FOCUS

Accounting opens doors to business, finance, and entrepreneurship. Our programme covers both the academic syllabus and practical financial literacy — skills that are useful for life, not just for exams.

  • High School Accounting — Grades 10, 11 & 12
  • Financial Literacy & Personal Finance
  • Trial Balance & the Accounting Equation
  • Income Statements & Balance Sheets
  • Cash Flow Statements & Budgets
  • Year-end adjustments & reconciliations
Real-World Skills: Beyond the textbook, we teach you how money actually works — budgeting, understanding payslips, and basic business accounts. Knowledge that stays with you for life.
